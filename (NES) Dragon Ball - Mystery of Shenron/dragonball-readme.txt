******************************
*Dragon Ball: Shenron no Nazo*
******************************

Dragon Ball: Shenron no Nazo is the first of many Dragon Ball games released for the Famicom, released in 1986.  It was also the only Dragon Ball game released outside of Japan, but it had its license stripped and heavily altered under the name "Dragon Power".  This game was also released in Europe with its license intact but in French.

The patch comes in two flavors...
Dragon Ball - Mystery of Shenron (English).ips
--English translation patch (IPS Format)
Dragon Ball - Mystery of Shenron (English).bps
--English translation patch (BPS Format)

The patch requires the ROM with the following hashes...
